==== How to Start ==== 

Double Click the Shimeji icon (Shimeji.exe).

==== How to quit ==== 

Right-click the tray icon of Shimeji, Select "Bye bye."

==== How to Uninstall ==== 

- From the installer -

Remove the Shimeji from the Add or Remove Programs in Control Panel.

- From the zip -

Delete the unzipped folder.

==== Source ====
